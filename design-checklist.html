The big picture

The good news: the bones of this app are genuinely excellent. The layout system, color foundation, and page structure are better than most apps at this stage — the reviewer compared the skeleton to Linear, one of the best-designed tools in the industry.

What's missing comes down to two themes: feedback (the app rarely tells you when something worked, failed, or is still loading) and consistency (a few places use their own one-off colors, sizes, and controls instead of the shared system). Close those two gaps and this goes from "well-built prototype" to "premium product." Most of it can be fixed in shared files, so one fix improves every page at once.
